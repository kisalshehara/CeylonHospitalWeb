At Ceylon Hospitals, we strive to provide an exceptional blend of modern amenities and the traditional essence of Sri Lankan indigenous medicine. Our facilities are designed to ensure a comfortable,and enriching experience for all our patients

Our commitment to quality healthcare, coupled with the rich heritage of Ayurveda, ensures that every patient receives personalized and effective treatment.

Additional Facilities

FacilitiesThat Make Us Unique


  • Modern Ayurvedic Equipments

    Incorporating the latest in medical technology, our hospital seamlessly integrates modern diagnostic and therapeutic equipment with the calming ambiance of Ayurvedic traditions. This fusion allows us to deliver precise medical care within a serene and healing environment.

  • Premium Ayurvedic Treatments

    Renowned for our comprehensive Ayurvedic treatments, Ceylon Hospitals offers a range of therapies that address various health needs. Our team, comprising over a thousand indigenous medical officers, consultants, and experts, provides individualized care using time-honored practices.

  • Ayurvedic Atmosphere

    Our hospital locations are thoughtfully designed to immerse you in the tranquil and rejuvenating atmosphere of Ayurveda. The architecture and interior design reflect the serenity and natural beauty associated with Ayurvedic wellness, creating a peaceful retreat from the stresses of everyday life.

  • Wellness Center

    Our wellness center is a sanctuary dedicated to holistic health and rejuvenation. It offers a range of services, including yoga, meditation, and therapeutic massages, all aimed at promoting physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

  • Aftercare Services

    Our aftercare services are designed to provide ongoing support and ensure lasting wellness. We offer personalized follow-up consultations, dietary and lifestyle advice, and continuous monitoring to help you maintain the benefits of your treatments.

  • Online Consulting Sessions

    Understanding the importance of accessibility, Ceylon Hospitals offers online consulting sessions with our esteemed Ayurvedic experts. This service enables patients to receive professional advice and guidance from the comfort of their homes, ensuring continuous care and support.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Explore our FAQ section to learn about Ceylon Hospitals, Sri Lanka's premier private hospital specializing in all indigenous medical systems. Find answers to common questions about our services, and the exceptional care provided by our team of over a thousand registered indigenous medical officers.

  • What is Ceylon Hospitals ?
    Ceylon Hospitals is a pioneering healthcare institution in Sri Lanka, renowned for being the first and only private hospital offering comprehensive services in all indigenous medical systems. We are a part of the Alpha Management Group (Pvt) Ltd., a reputed entity in the international business world.
  • What indigenous medical systems are available at Ceylon Hospitals?
    We offer treatments across all Sri Lankan indigenous medical systems, including Siddha, Unani, Ayurveda, and Traditional medicine. These systems have been practiced for thousands of years and are known for their unique therapeutic methods.
  • Can foreign patients receive treatment at Ceylon Hospitals?
    Yes, foreign patients are welcome at Ceylon Hospitals. We provide high-quality medical services, along with accommodation and other facilities to ensure a comfortable stay for our international patients. We also offer supportive services like language translation to assist our foreign guests.
  • What makes Ceylon Hospitals unique?
    Ceylon Hospitals is unique for its exclusive focus on Sri Lankan indigenous medicine. We are the only hospital that integrates all indigenous medical systems under one roof and brings this rich heritage to the global stage. Additionally, our collaboration with lecturers from Sri Lankan universities of indigenous medicine and traditional physicians enhances the quality and authenticity of our services.
  • How do I make an appointment at Ceylon Hospitals?
    You can make an appointment by contacting our hospital directly through our website or customer service hotline. We will guide you through the process and help you schedule a consultation with the appropriate medical officer.
